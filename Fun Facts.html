Another fun fact is that my Appendix ruptured inside of me when I was 11. I almost died but they figured out what had happened just in time. I went to school that day because I felt better, and when the doctor called to check in and my mom told him that he called me in to the hospital immediately. It turns out if you go from really bad to fine in a short period it is very likely the euphoria before you die. He was right it had ruptured that morning. Now it is a cool story to tell.

Another fun fact is that I am missing nine teeth. I have a very small mouth and only three molars. I did have one wisdom tooth though, just one. They say it is a generational thing that because of the differences in the minerals in the water and the the difference in how food is processed that new generations will be missing the same teeth as me. I was one of the first of the generation to be missing them but it has become really common in age groups younger then me.
